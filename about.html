ABOUT

I wrote my first erotic story at twelve — and I never really stopped. Growing up in Brazil as a queer woman, I carried a lot of shame around my sexuality, so I vowed to become someone who could normalize desire the way I wish someone had for me. Since moving to Berlin in 2016, I've created sex-positive content across mediums — from scripts and audio for brands like Ersties and Femtasy, to video content, to workshops, live experiences, and public speaking. I co-created I Am The Clitoris, a project about clitoral anatomy and pleasure, and I've recently expanded into advocacy, fighting censorship and championing sexual wellness. My work is feminist, queer-inclusive, and consent-centered — because pleasure is personal, political, and transformative.
